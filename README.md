**MiCrochet - Handmade Crochet Creations**

**Project Overview**
MiCrochet is a responsive, modern e-commerce website for a handmade crochet business specializing in custom and ready-made crochet items. The website serves as a digital storefront that showcases the artistry and craftsmanship of handmade crochet products while providing an engaging shopping experience.

**Purpose**
**Showcase Artistry:** Display beautiful handmade crochet products in an elegant digital environment
**Business Growth:** Enable customers to discover, customize, and order crochet items online
**Brand Storytelling:** Share the passion and craftsmanship behind each handmade piece
**Customer Engagement:** Provide an intuitive platform for custom orders and inquiries

**Key Features
Feature	Description	Benefit:**
- Responsive Design	Fully optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile.	- Accessible shopping experience across all devices
- Product Catalog	Organized display of crochet categories with filtering. - Easy product discovery and browsing
- Interactive Gallery	Visual portfolio with hover effects. - Showcases craftsmanship and inspires customers
- Feedback Slider. - Auto-rotating customer reviews with navigation
                   -	Builds trust and social proof
- Shopping Cart Simulation. - Add-to-cart functionality with counter
                            - Familiar e-commerce experience
- Custom Order System. - Dedicated contact form for personalized requests
                       - Enables bespoke creations
- Smooth Animations. - Scroll-triggered animations and transitions
                     - Enhanced user engagement
- Mobile Navigation	- Hamburger menu with smooth animations	Seamless mobile experience

**Product Categories**
**Amigurumi Toys** - Handcrafted stuffed animals and characters

**Winter Accessories** - Hats, scarves, and gloves
